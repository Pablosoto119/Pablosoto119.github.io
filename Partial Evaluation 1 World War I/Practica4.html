Battles of Ypres WW1








Modern historiography on the Battle of Ypres remains firmly rooted in the official histories written by Great Britain, France and Germany.
The historiography of the third Ypres remains particularly controversial. Contemporary writings emphasize the complexity of the battle, but there is disagreement as to whether the British forces carried out the attack correctly. (S. Jones, 2015)
